VERISMO

Techno - Cultural fest of MCA Dept of LMCST

JOURNEY TO THE TECH VERSE



VERISMO meaning 'Realism' is an annual Techno-Cultural programme for encouraging and promoting innovations from all Students in the state . Verismo has been conducted by the MCA department of LOURDES MATHA COLLEGE OF SCIENCE AND TECHNOLOGY.

VERISMO is a collaborative effort of all students and teachers of MCA department. We thank all those who take their effort to bring this program to a great success.
